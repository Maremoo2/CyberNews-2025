# Cybersecurity Incidents – January 2025

## Global Overview

### Trends & Analysis

January 2025 marked the beginning of an unprecedented year for cybersecurity threats. The month saw 590 ransomware attacks globally, with North America and Europe as primary targets. Cryptocurrency exchanges faced significant threats as organized cybercriminals, particularly groups linked to North Korea's Lazarus Group, demonstrated increasing sophistication in targeting major digital-asset platforms.

The month set the tone for 2025's cyber landscape, with large-scale attacks affecting multiple sectors including finance, healthcare, and public infrastructure. Security experts noted the growing boldness of nation-state-backed cybercrime operations, with tools from espionage campaigns being repurposed for profit-driven attacks.

## Major Global Incidents

### Jan 15, 2025 – Phemex Cryptocurrency Exchange Hack

- **Attack Type:** Cryptocurrency exchange breach
- **Impact:** At least $69 million stolen; widespread service disruption
- **Attribution:** Suspected links to Lazarus Group (North Korea)
- **Details:** Singapore-based crypto platform Phemex was breached, with attackers stealing at least $69 million. The hack forced Phemex to halt withdrawals and deposits while assessing damage. An investigation found hackers compromised Phemex's systems, leading to the loss of user funds and widespread service disruption. This incident underscored the continuing threat of large-scale cryptocurrency thefts.
- **Source:** [The Record - At least $69 million stolen from crypto platform Phemex](https://therecord.media)

### Jan 15, 2025 – Global Ransomware Surge

- **Attack Type:** Ransomware (multiple groups)
- **Impact:** 590 ransomware attacks globally, marking the start of an unprecedented year
- **Attribution:** Various ransomware groups
- **Details:** January saw 590 ransomware attacks globally, marking the start of an unprecedented year for cyber threats with North America and Europe as primary targets. The surge in activity demonstrated the continuing evolution of ransomware-as-a-service operations and the increasing sophistication of threat actors.
- **Source:** [NCC Group - Monthly Threat Pulse](https://www.nccgroup.com)

## Statistics

- **590 ransomware attacks** recorded globally in January 2025
- **$69 million+** stolen in the Phemex cryptocurrency hack
- **North America and Europe** were primary targets for ransomware attacks

- **Healthcare and finance sectors** among the most frequently targeted

## Emerging Threats

- **Nation-state crypto targeting:** Increased focus by state-backed groups on cryptocurrency platforms
- **Ransomware evolution:** Growing sophistication in ransomware-as-a-service operations
- **Supply chain vulnerabilities:** Continued exploitation of third-party service providers
